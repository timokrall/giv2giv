Frequently Asked Questions

Advantages of giv2giv

Why not just donate directly to my charity?

  • With giv2giv charities can receive a lot more money in the long-run
  • giv2giv lets you maintain advisory control over the funds in your funds even after you have donated
  • If you wish, your donation can remain anonymous—no more marketing waste!

How do donors benefit?

  • Leave a legacy that will support your favorite charities forever, not just a one-time donation
  • Build, share and grow endowments with friends, family, and co-workers
  • Donations are tax deductible to the same extent as a normal donations to your favorite charity

What are the advantages for charities that use giv2giv?

  • Source regular, predictable unrestricted donations for your organization’s most important needs
  • Spend less money on marketing designed to acquire and retain donors
  • Reduced administrative burden - no gift receipts to send

The giv2giv Philosophy

Our founding mission

Our mission is to revolutionize personal philanthropy by democratizing donor-advised funds.

giv2giv.org is an online fundraising platform that allows people to donate, regardless of donation size, to endowments which provide non-profit organizations with a perpetual income stream of unrestricted grants. Donations can be made to existing endowments or a donor can choose to create their own unique endowment to include the variety of causes which they support. Grants are paid to charities on a quarterly basis and charities are allowed to use the funds for any charitable purpose.

Our goals are simple:

  1. Provide consistent, recurring revenue to 501(c)(3) charities
  2. Reduce administrative overhead
  3. Allow all donors and charities to collaborate on the transition from episodic giving to a legacy of generational wealth for good

Technical Questions

How does my donation get to my favorite causes?

giv2giv uses a Donor Advised Fund (DAF) as its underlying charitable giving and investment vehicle. DAFs were originally approved by the IRS in the 1930s and are now the fastest growing charitable giving vehicle in the United States of America, with more than 200,000 donor-advised accounts established holding over $45 billion in assets.

Donor Advised Funds allow donors to maintain advisory rights to their donations, so you tell giv2giv where your donations should go through your donor “advice” (the ‘A’ in Donor Advised Fund). At giv2giv you specify this advice by creating or support a fund that grants to your favorite charities.

By law DAFs must be sponsored by a nonprofit organization. The giv2giv Donor Advised Fund sponsor is giv2giv, Inc. a Virginia nonprofit incorporated in June 2012.

What is the investment performance of giv2giv?

The past and projected investment performance of the funds invested with giv2giv is openly disclosed. We have chosen a conservative investment strategy that aims to preserve the principal while growing enough to cover periodical grants. In the past, the total funds grew steadily over time.

Is subscribing a long-term commitment?

No, you have full flexibility. After you subscribe to a fund, you will see our green “Subscribe” button turn into a red “Unsubscribe” button. Click that button and you are unsubscribed with no additional payments or fees.

Are there any fees?

giv2giv is committed to sending as much as possible to the charities we support. We did a fee analysis that compares giv2giv to other charitable investment vehicles, and we are proud to say giv2giv fares very well.

giv2giv keeps the lights on with a 0.125% per quarter fee on the balance of the fund. On an annual basis this is one half of one percent which we feel is more than a fair deal for the services provided. In fact, we think this is as low or lower than any Donor Advised Fund or private foundation out there.

The credit card processor (stripe.com) charges a fee to send money from your credit card to giv2giv—this is currently $0.30 plus 2.9% per donation. This fee is deducted from your donation prior to being added to your balance in the giv2giv fund.

Can I support giv2giv?

You have an idea how to support giv2giv? giv2giv is open-source so you can contribute by coding, designing, and adding further low-cost payment processors. Join us. Let’s work together.
