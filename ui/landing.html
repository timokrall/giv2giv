How giv2giv works

  Step 1: You donate to an endowment

Support an existing charitable endowment fund on giv2giv or create your own. You can select whether your donations are charged from your credit card or drafted from your bank account. In any case, your charitable giving with giv2giv is tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law. For your convenience, all your donations are summarized in one single donation receipt.

giv2giv is a tax-exempt 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization registered in the United States.

  Step 2: giv2giv invests your donations

The giv2giv platform tracks and manages the funds you create within the giv2giv fund. Donations are invested in the Pax World Balanced Fund, a leading Socially Responsible Investment (SRI) mutual fund. All investment gains and losses are tracked at the individual donor level.

We believe in transparency, collaboration, and are a proud part of the open source movement. Our methodology is 100% transparent—you can read our open-source code any time.

   Step 3: Charities of your choice receive grants

giv2giv makes periodic grants to donors’ favorite causes, currently 1.25% of the entire fund every 90 days for a total annual grant of about 5% per year.

We send anonymous, unrestricted grants to charities so they can direct resources where they are most needed. With giv2giv you have minimal effort and maximum impact.
